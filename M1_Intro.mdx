**Aura** is an **AI‑powered speech analytics platform** designed to turn every customer interaction into a source of actionable insight. 

It provides deep insights into call recordings, enabling comprehensive analysis, automated call scoring, and performance tracking. 

Aura enables organisations to - 

- **Identify Success Drivers**: See which agent behaviours lead to positive outcomes. 
- **Spot Coaching Opportunities**: Pinpoint areas where agents excel or need additional support. 
- **Ensure Consistent Compliance**: Automatically flag policy or script deviations. 
- **Boost Performance and Satisfaction**: Turn insights into measurable and actionable improvements. 

Aura turns every recording into a clear analysis and scores. This guide gives you easy-to-follow instructions for using Aura's features, from the first setup steps to more advanced tools.

Let's see how Aura can help you reach your business goals.
